On page http://10.12.248.13/index.php?page=recover we right click->inspect element. We found that there's a text object that's hidden with a mail:
<input type="hidden" name="mail" value="webmaster@borntosec.com" maxlength="15">

We switch 'hidden' to 'text'
<input type="text" name="mail" value="webmaster@borntosec.com" maxlength="15">

We then empty it and press submit, then we get the flag.

Well, for this one, dont leave an adress hidden in the html.
