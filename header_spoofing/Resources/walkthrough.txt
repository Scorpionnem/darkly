We open the sourcepage with CTRL+U. We see many comments including ones saying that we must come from https://www.nsa.gov/ and another one saying we need to be on browser ft_bornToSec.
We used a curl command with 2 headers, one for referer and one for the user-agent which is our browser:
curl -H "User-Agent: ft_bornToSec" -H "Referer: https://www.nsa.gov/" 'http://10.12.248.13/index.php?page=b7e44c7a40c5f80139f0a50f3650fb2bd8d00b0d24667c4c2ca32c88e13b758f'

We then get the flag in the response.
